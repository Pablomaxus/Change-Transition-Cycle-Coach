# CHANGE TRANSITION CYCLE COACH

Individual transition is the cornerstone of organizational transformation, yet leaders often rely on rigid frameworks that ignore human emotion. The **Change Transition Cycle Coach** helps change leaders to implement a dynamic road map, based on the five-stage Kübler-Ross Change Curve:

    Stage 1. Shock and Denial, 
    Stage 2. Frustration and Anger, 
    Stage 3. Depression and Confusion, 
    Stage 4. Experimentation and Acceptance, 
    Stage 5. Integration and Commitment
    
Because individuals navigate these psychological stages at varying speeds and in non-linear trajectories, static management strategies often fail or trigger regressions. 

By coaching change managers to accurately diagnose an individual's emotional state, it is possible to deploy tailored communication and support mechanisms that effectively mitigate resistance, prevent stagnation, and accelerate the transition from denial to full integration.


## What this is

This folder structure implements a modular prompt engineering architecture built on the Interpretable Context Methodology. Instead of relying on a single, oversized prompt, this framework breaks down the operational intelligence of an AI specialist into five distinct files where each file executes exactly one specialized role. 

The system operates as an interactive, portable coaching tool that runs within a context-isolated workspace to transform the AI model into a dedicated advisory partner rather than a generic document retriever.

---

## Who it's for

This project is designed specifically for change leaders or organizational managers who are responsible for building adaptive capacity, managing human resistance, and guiding teams through complex workplace transitions.

---

## How it adapts to you

The coach does not deliver generic lectures or static lists of management theories. It dynamically calibrates its interactive feedback by analyzing your real-time leadership challenges against the specific behavioral and psychological markers of the five change transition stages. By listening to your day-to-day scenarios, the coach adjusts its questioning frameworks and pushback intensity to address the exact resistance level and emotional state of your team.

---

## How to use it

To deploy the specialist, upload the entire folder containing the Markdown files into your AI workspace or project environment. Once the context is loaded, the system instantly assumes its training parameters. You can begin the session by describing a current organizational transition, detailing a specific team conflict, or requesting a pre-action review for an upcoming high-stakes meeting.

---

## Example opening

You can provide a large opening statement with detailed context, or a simple entry line and the coach will lead you through a discovery process.

* My department is transitioning to a new enterprise software platform next month, and several senior team members are completely ignoring training announcements and continuing to use legacy systems. I need a clear strategy for our next team alignment meeting.
* Following our recent restructuring announcement, my direct reports have become highly critical during stand-up meetings, and productivity has dropped. I want to address their root fears without getting defensive or shutting down their feedback.
* I have an issue with a team member.
* Several team members are refusing to engage..

---

## The hedge

This system is explicitly architected to avoid functioning as a standard knowledge base. It rejects passive text retrieval and avoids delivering textbook summaries or superficial recommendations. Guided by strict operational boundaries, the coach prioritizes rigorous pushback, deep discovery, and behavior tracking. It actively challenges your management assumptions, maps team resistance patterns, and requires specific execution benchmarks to ensure true professional accountability between interactions.
